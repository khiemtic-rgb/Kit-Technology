## Novixa in one sentence

**Novixa** is cloud pharmacy management software — POS, inventory, batch & expiry, reporting, CRM, and **AI Assistant** — for single stores and chains.

Product site: [novixa.vn](https://novixa.vn/en)

## Who is it for?

- Pharmacies moving from spreadsheets to a real system
- Chains that need **central governance** across branches
- Owners who need **real-time reporting** and GPP compliance control

## Core modules

| Module | Value |
|---|---|
| POS | Fast checkout, barcode scan, receipts |
| Inventory | Stock levels, movements, alerts |
| Batch & expiry | Reduce expired stock risk |
| Reporting | Revenue, margin, branch KPIs |
| Customer app | Orders, loyalty, refill reminders |
| AI Assistant | Lookup and consultation support |
| API | Third-party integrations |

## Novixa and KIT Technology

**KIT Technology** is the parent company — product, engineering, and platform operations. **Novixa** is the dedicated product brand for pharmacy retail.
