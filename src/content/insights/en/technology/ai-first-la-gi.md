## AI First is not a buzzword

**AI First** means every product decision — data architecture, APIs, UX, operations — considers **where AI creates value**, instead of bolting AI on after the software is done.

## Three layers at KIT

### 1. Product

Novixa AI Assistant, smart suggestions, document lookup — AI is a **core capability**, not an add-on.

### 2. Process

AI assists coding, review, spec summarization, testing — faster shipping with human approval.

### 3. Operations

Monitoring, ticket classification, automated reporting — less repetitive work.

## AI-washing vs real AI First

| AI-washing | Real AI First |
|---|---|
| Generic chatbot | Domain-grounded AI |
| No measurement | Clear KPIs |
| Black box | Controlled outputs, audit |

## Related stack choices

- **PostgreSQL** — structured data for reporting and RAG
- **API First** — one source of truth for apps and AI
- **Cloud Native** — SaaS-scale elasticity

## Conclusion

AI First is a **product discipline**, not marketing fluff. KIT Technology applies it from Novixa to internal engineering — always with human accountability.
