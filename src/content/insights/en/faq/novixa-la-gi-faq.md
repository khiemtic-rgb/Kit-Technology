## What is Novixa?

**Novixa** is a cloud pharmacy management platform built by **KIT Technology**. It covers POS checkout, inventory, batch and expiry tracking, business reporting, customer CRM, and an AI assistant.

## How is Novixa different from novixa.vn?

- **novixa.vn** — product marketing and documentation site
- **Novixa software** — the SaaS app you log into to run daily pharmacy operations

## Who builds it?

**KIT Technology** (kittech.vn) — an AI-first software company — builds and operates the platform. Novixa is the vertical product brand for pharmacy retail.
