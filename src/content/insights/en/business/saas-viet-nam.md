## SaaS is mainstream now

Vietnamese businesses increasingly pay **monthly subscriptions** instead of one-time licenses — especially when they need continuous updates, security, and support.

## Tailwinds

- Cloud infrastructure is widely available
- Online payments and e-invoicing
- SMBs willing to try vertical software
- AI lowers product development and operations cost

## Local challenges

1. **Data trust** — where data lives and who can access it
2. **Customization vs standardization**
3. **B2B sales cycles** — demos and case studies matter
4. **Price pressure** — comparison with Excel or legacy “free” tools

## KIT Technology strategy

We focus on **vertical SaaS** — starting with pharmacies via **Novixa**, then AI automation and enterprise platforms — rather than generic software for every industry.

## Product lessons

- **Land** on a clear pain (POS + inventory + compliance)
- **Expand** with chains, customer apps, AI
- **Retain** with support, training, and measurable value

## Conclusion

SaaS in Vietnam is a **major opportunity** for product teams with deep domain knowledge — KIT Technology bets on knowledge plus software, not project outsourcing alone.
