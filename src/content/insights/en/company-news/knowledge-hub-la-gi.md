## Why a Knowledge Hub?

A product company website should not stop at marketing pages. **Insights** is where KIT shares practical knowledge on software, AI, and pharmacy operations — so prospects can learn before they reach out.

## The 90-day goal

We plan to publish about **150 articles** across:

- **Company** — eight foundational pages already live
- **Technology** — stack and engineering practices
- **Solutions** — healthcare and pharmacy transformation
- **Products / Novixa** — guides and comparisons
- **Insights** — AI, engineering, and business
- **FAQ** — common questions

## Create once, publish everywhere

Each article can be repurposed into short videos, carousels, email, LinkedIn, Facebook, and more through our content automation pipeline.
