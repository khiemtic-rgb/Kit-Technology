## 2026 as a turning point

Pharmacies in Vietnam no longer compete on location and price alone. Customers expect **online lookup, fast ordering, and app-based support** — while chain owners need **centralized metrics** to decide.

## Three pillars

### 1. Digital operations

Modern POS, inventory with batch and expiry control, real-time reporting. Goal: **fewer errors, faster stocktake**.

### 2. Customer experience

Customer apps, messaging channels, loyalty, refill reminders. Goal: **retention and repeat revenue**.

### 3. Governance & compliance

GPP, roles, audit trails, multi-branch dashboards. Goal: **scale without losing control**.

## AI is here now

AI helps with drug lookup, sensible cross-sell suggestions, and FAQ handling — **freeing pharmacists** for deeper consultation.

## Common mistakes

- Many small tools with no integration
- Fast rollout without staff training
- Digitizing sales only, ignoring inventory and compliance

## Where to start

1. Standardize core flows (receive — sell — stocktake)
2. Pick **one platform** that scales to chains
3. Track KPIs: stock, expiry, revenue per customer, service time
